Sonia Hashim

Hi there! I’m Sonia and I’m a masters student in the CS Dept at UCSB and President of WiCS. I’m interested in media ecosystems for content creation and distribution. This fall, I’m working with the Expressive Computation Lab with Prof. Jennifer Jacobs in the MAT (Media, Arts & Tech) Dept. to build a novel animation system. In my spare time, I like to read, get outdoors, interact with and learn about all kinds of media, and I’m always up for talking about it and meeting new people. I love to travel (45+ countries, 5 stans is the next trip on my list), spend time by the sea, and I just really like sharks. I hope the WiCS community grows over time to encourage and support diversity and engagement in tech across the board at UCSB.
